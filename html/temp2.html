Accomplish Agriculture Circular

Below is our frame of circular agriculture. Cellulolytic enzyme initially decomposes the rice straw to synthesize saccharification liquid, which can incubate the Bacillus subtilis RM125/PQQ . It could turn the saccharification liquid to more valuable biostimulants that can significantly foster the rice to grow. Through all the steps in this system, nothing was wasted, which can successfully verify that circular agriculture is accomplishable. For more experiments detail see our Result page.
